Explore our tours

Salalah City Tour

Explore Salalah city, with a visit to the Museum of the Frankincense Land, on this private, half-day tour. You’ll also visit Qaboos Mosque, the frankincense market, and have plenty of time to explore the souks.

East Salalah Tour

Craving an adventurous cultural odyssey? East Salalah awaits! Conquer the dizzying Zig-Zag Road, explore ancient Mirbat Port, and kayak through Wadi Darbat's emerald paradise. Dive into history at Al Mughsail Beach, then trade spices in buzzing souks. Bedouin feasts, Omani rhythms, and turquoise dips in Darbat waterfall complete this epic escape. Unforgettable adventure awaits

West Salalah Tour

Ditch the city's stress and lifestyle and connect with nature on a half-day tour. where you visit the Ayn Razat's beautiful natural spring water. After, walk the shores of Mughsail beach to watch the sculptured cliffs and holes from limestone rocks. Visit the Marnif cave and the lagoon to watch the flamingos.

Full day East and West Salalah Tour

Forget choosing between sun-kissed shores and rugged mountains – this full-day East and West Salalah tour lets you embrace both! Dive into the turquoise paradise of Mughsail Beach, snorkel with vibrant coral reefs, and build sandcastles kissed by the Arabian Sea. As the sun climbs higher, conquer the legendary Zig-Zag Road, carving its way through dramatic cliffs with breathtaking panoramas.

Desert Sunset

Our desert sunset tour whisks you through endless dunes on thrilling camelback rides. Conquer soaring cliffs, kayak through hidden wadis, and lose yourself in the rhythm of ancient souks. As the sun paints the sky in fiery hues, share nomadic feasts and laughter under a million stars.

Desert Overnight

Ditch the beach chair for camel treks and campfire feasts! Trade sunsets for a million-star lullaby on Salalah's sands. This overnight desert escape whispers ancient secrets with the wind, paints adventures in fire, and leaves you transformed under the Milky Way's symphony.

We orchestrate unforgettable experiences, leaving visitors forever enchanted by this desert oasis and its warm-hearted people
